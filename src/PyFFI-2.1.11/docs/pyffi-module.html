    • pyffi.utils.tristrip: A wrapper for TriangleStripifier and some utility functions, for stripification of sets of triangles, stitching and unstitching strips, and triangulation of strips.
    • pyffi.utils.vertex_cache: Algorithms to reorder triangle list order and vertex order aiming to minimize vertex cache misses.
